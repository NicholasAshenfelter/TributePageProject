A Tribute to Vic Mignogna

Vic Mignogna


This page is dedicated to Vic Mignogna, a respected and well known voice actor. Vic has done voice work on a number of animated properties. His most notable works are for the English dubbed versions of animes such as Dragon Ball Z and Fullmetal Alchemist.
